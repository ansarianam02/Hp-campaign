ABOUT THE CONTEST
Making an impact towards sustainaibility

Continuing our Sustainable Impact journey, we bring to you the ‘Race to Space’ collage contest. Through this initiative, we want to hear your thoughts and see your ideas on how to fight inequality and injustice, end extreme poverty and tackle climate change – some of world’s most pressing issues!


Pick one of the nine Global Goals [link to topics] linked to HP’s Sustainable Impact strategy, and compose your message on a collage. This contest is your chance to express, in the most creative ways, how we can move closer to achieving the set goals. At the same time, you stand to win amazing prizes!!!


Learn About Sustainable Development Goals by visiting https://sustainabledevelopment.un.org
